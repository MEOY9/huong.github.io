Graduate student in University of Maryland at College Park

I'm a biophysics PhD candidate under the supervision of Dr. Thirumalai (for his old group website, go here.) My current PhD research focuses on developing models to analyze and interpret single molecule experiments, in order to discover new general principles of biological motors.
